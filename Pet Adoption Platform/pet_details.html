Next Steps for Your Pet Adoption

You're almost there!

Now that you've selected your pet, here are the next steps to complete the adoption process:

  1. Complete the Adoption Application:

    Fill out the adoption application form. This helps us understand your lifestyle and ensure the pet is a good fit for you.

  2. Schedule a Meet-and-Greet:

    Once we receive your application, we will contact you to arrange a meet-and-greet with the pet. This gives you a chance to interact and make sure it’s the right match.

  3. Home Visit (if needed):

    For some adoptions, we may request a brief home visit to make sure your living environment is safe for the pet.

  4. Pay the Adoption Fee:

    After the meet-and-greet, you'll need to pay the adoption fee. This covers medical costs, vaccinations, and care the pet received at our shelter.

  5. Finalizing the Adoption:

    Once the paperwork is completed and the adoption fee is paid, you’ll officially be able to take your new pet home!

If you have any questions during the process, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help!
